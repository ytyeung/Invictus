INVICTUS

Invictus System was built during the internship at CUHK.

In the Canteen located at Chinese University of Hong Kong, a deaf woman was employed at the drink dispensing area as a staff facilitating the production. However, it is hard for customers to communicate with her, only way to communicate is by hand signs and through body language .

 

This encouraged the idea of using sound recognition technology to encourage people with physical disabilities participate as our labour force of our society, such as hear impairment.

INSPIRATION

SOFTWARES AND HARDWARES REQUIRED

 

Software used:

1. Julius4 (Sound-Recognition)

2. WxPython (GUI)

 

Hardware required: 

1. Raspberry Pi

2. Microphone

3. Monitor

4. Audio card(For input and Output)

5. Mouse and Keyboard

6. Mechanic button(Optional)

IDEA

We intend to use a Raspberry Pi as a small computer to carry out the task of Sound Recognition.

The Following is the flow map of our idea:

 

1. A Raspberry Pi is installed at the food order place

2. Customers order through the microphone attached to the Pi within a time limit..

3. The order will be read by the computer and turned into words on the screen.

4. Customers can reconfirm their order.

5. The workers will know what types of food and drinks need to be prepared!
